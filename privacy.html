Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 4, 2026 | Last Updated: January 4, 2026

1. Introduction

Oodles & Doodles ("we," "us," "our") operates oodlesanddoodles.app (the "Service"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with:

  • Australian Privacy Act 1988 and Australian Privacy Principles (APPs)
  • Australian Online Dating Code of Conduct (effective October 2024)
  • UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

Our Service is strictly for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, password, display name
  • Profile Information: Age, location, position preference, bio, profile photos
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Stripe (we do not store card details)
  • Communications: Messages sent through our platform
  • Support Requests: Information provided when contacting support

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: IP address, device type, browser type, operating system
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent on Service
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address (not precise GPS)
  • Cookies: See our Cookie Policy

2.3 Information From Third Parties

  • Google Sign-In: If you use Google authentication, we receive your name and email from Google

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Create and manage your account
  • Enable you to connect with other users
  • Process payments and subscriptions
  • Send service-related communications
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and safety issues
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Improve and develop our Service

Legal Basis (GDPR)

  • Contract Performance: To provide services you've subscribed to
  • Legitimate Interests: Service improvement, fraud prevention, safety
  • Consent: Where required by law (you can withdraw consent anytime)
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with laws and regulations

4. How We Share Your Information

4.1 With Other Users

  • Your profile (name, age, location, bio, photos) is visible to other users
  • Your messages are visible to recipients
  • We will NEVER share your email address with other users

4.2 Service Providers

  • Firebase: Authentication and database hosting
  • Stripe: Payment processing
  • Netlify: Website hosting

These providers are bound by confidentiality obligations.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by:

  • Court orders or legal process
  • Australian eSafety Commissioner or law enforcement
  • Emergency situations involving safety threats
  • Enforcement of our Terms of Service

4.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale, your information may be transferred to the new owner with notice to you.

5. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in:

  • Australia
  • United States (Firebase, Stripe servers)
  • Other countries where our service providers operate

We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers as required by applicable laws.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Australian Users (Privacy Act)

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Complaint: Lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC)

6.2 UK/EU Users (GDPR)

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion ("right to be forgotten")
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in portable format
  • Object: Object to certain processing
  • Restrict Processing: Request limits on how we use your data
  • Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on consent

6.3 California Users (CCPA)

  • Know: What personal information we collect and how we use it
  • Delete: Request deletion of your information
  • Opt-Out: We do not sell personal information
  • Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate for exercising your rights

6.4 All Users Can:

  • Update profile information directly in the app
  • Delete your account at any time
  • Opt out of marketing emails (via unsubscribe link)
  • Request your data or deletion by emailing support@oodlesanddoodles.app

We will respond to requests within:

  • Australia: 30 days
  • UK/EU: 1 month (extendable by 2 months for complex requests)
  • California: 45 days

7. Data Retention

We retain your information:

  • Active Accounts: For as long as your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: 90 days after deletion (to allow recovery)
  • Messages: Automatically deleted after 30 days (premium users can disable)
  • Legal Requirements: Longer if required by law or for legitimate purposes

After these periods, data is permanently deleted from our systems.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: Data encrypted in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest
  • Firebase Security: Authentication and database security rules
  • Access Controls: Limited employee access to personal data
  • Regular Audits: Security reviews and updates

However, no system is 100% secure. You're responsible for keeping your password confidential.

9. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our Service is NOT intended for anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

If we discover we've collected information from someone under 18:

  • We will delete it immediately
  • We will terminate the account
  • Parents/guardians can contact us at support@oodlesanddoodles.app

10. Australian Online Dating Code Compliance

In accordance with the Australia Online Dating Code of Conduct:

  • We implement systems to detect and respond to online harm
  • We provide reporting mechanisms for harmful content
  • We moderate content according to community guidelines
  • We publish transparency reports on safety measures
  • We respond to reports within required timeframes

  • Preferences: Remember your settings
  • Analytics: Understand how users interact with our Service
  • Security: Prevent fraud and abuse

You can control cookies through your browser settings. See our Cookie Policy for details.
