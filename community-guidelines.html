Community Guidelines

Welcome to Oodles & Doodles! Our community is built on respect, safety, and authentic connections. Please follow these guidelines to help create a positive experience for everyone.

1. Be Respectful

  • Treat others the way you want to be treated
  • Respect boundaries and consent at all times
  • No harassment, hate speech, or discriminatory language
  • Respect different opinions, backgrounds, and identities

2. Be Authentic

  • Use real photos of yourself
  • Provide accurate information in your profile
  • Don't impersonate others or create fake accounts
  • Be honest about your intentions

3. Stay Safe

  • Don't share personal information (address, financial details) too soon
  • Meet in public places for first dates
  • Trust your instincts - if something feels wrong, it probably is
  • Report suspicious behavior or profiles

4. Keep It Appropriate

  • No nudity or sexually explicit content in public profiles
  • Don't send unsolicited explicit messages or images
  • Keep conversations respectful and consensual
  • No spam or commercial solicitation

5. Report Violations

If you encounter behavior that violates these guidelines:

  • Use the report button on profiles or messages
  • Block users who make you uncomfortable
  • Contact our support team with evidence
Zero Tolerance Policy: We have zero tolerance for violence, threats, harassment, hate speech, or illegal activity. Violations will result in immediate account termination and may be reported to authorities.

6. Consequences

Violating these guidelines may result in:

  • Warning from our moderation team
  • Temporary account suspension
  • Permanent account ban
  • Legal action for serious violations
