Refund Policy

Last updated: 13 January 2026

This Refund Policy applies to purchases made through the Oodles & Doodles application and website (oodlesanddoodles.app). Please read it carefully before making a purchase.

1. Digital products & subscriptions

Unless required by law, all sales of digital products, in‑app purchases, and subscriptions are non‑refundable once access has been granted or content has been delivered.

If you experience technical issues that prevent you from accessing what you purchased, contact us within 7 days so we can investigate and assist.

2. Duplicate or unauthorised charges

You may be eligible for a refund if:

  • You have been charged more than once for the same item.
  • You see a payment that you believe is unauthorised or made in error.

In these cases, please contact us as soon as possible with your order details so we can review and, if appropriate, issue a refund or credit.

3. Subscription cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time. When you cancel, you will continue to have access to paid features until the end of your current billing period.

Cancelling a subscription does not automatically trigger a refund for the remaining time in the billing cycle unless required by applicable law.

4. How to request a refund

To request a refund or query a charge, please contact:

Email: support@oodlesanddoodles.app
Subject line: Refund Request – Oodles & Doodles

Include the following information in your request:

  • Your full name and the email address used in the app.
  • Date of purchase and transaction ID (if available).
  • A short description of the issue and the reason for your request.

5. Processing time

Once your request is received, we aim to review it within 5–10 business days. If a refund is approved, it will be processed using the original payment method. Your bank or payment provider may take additional time to post the credit.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date. Continued use of Oodles & Doodles after changes are posted means you accept the revised policy.

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at support@oodlesanddoodles.app.
